## What is Bhindi? A Quick Introduction

<Card title="Your Smartest AI Teammate" icon="robot">
  Bhindi is your smartest teammate. You just tell it what you need in plain
  English, and it gets it done. Whether it's drafting an email, pulling info
  from Notion, summarizing a doc, scheduling something on your calendar, or
  updating a spreadsheet, Bhindi talks to all your apps and takes care of the
  busywork for you.
</Card>

<Note>
  **No more tab switching, no more context switching** - just talk to Bhindi and
  move on with your important work.
</Note>

### Popular Integrations

<Accordion title="Google Workspace">
  - **Gmail**: Send, read, and manage emails 
  - **Google Calendar**: Schedule meetings and events 
  - **Google Drive**: Access and manage files 
  - **Google Sheets**: Update and analyze spreadsheets
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Microsoft 365">
  - **Outlook**: Email management and scheduling 
  - **OneDrive**: File storage and sharing 
  - **Teams**: Communication and collaboration 
  - **Excel**: Data analysis and reporting
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Development Tools">
  - **GitHub**: Repository management and CI/CD 
  - **Notion**: Documentation and knowledge management 
  - **Slack**: Team communication and notifications 
  - **YouTube**: Video analysis and content extraction
</Accordion>

<Tip>
  You can instantly use agents for tasks like scheduling, summarizing, searching

  integrating with popular apps.
